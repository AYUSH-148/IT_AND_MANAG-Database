Top MBA Colleges in India 2024

With the release of NIRF Rankings 2023 in June 2023, the Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs) & Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) have emerged as the top MBA colleges in India once again. Barring IIMs & IITs, ISB Hyderabad, XLRI-Xavier School of Management Jamshedpur, Management Development Institute Gurgaon, Symbiosis Institute of Business Management Pune, S.P. Jain Institute of Management & Research Mumbai, etc. are amongst the top MBA colleges in India 2023.

Many of the MBA colleges have managed to retain their positions as compared to NIRF Rankings 2022, with a few new entrants on the list. Taking admission to top MBA colleges in India is extremely difficult with the number of MBA aspirants increasing year on year. Luckily, there are many top private MBA colleges in India today, to cater to the increasing demand for Management education in the country.

Admission to most MBA colleges is offered on the basis of an entrance test, followed by a Group Discussion (GD), Personal Interview (PI), and Written Aptitude Test (WAT). Some of the most popular MBA entrance exams in India include CAT, MAT, CMAT, NMAT by GMAC, XAT, SNAP, GMAT, MAH MBA CET, IIFT exam, etc. Check out the important details about top MBA colleges in India 2023 that are going to help you pick the right one!

Top MBA Colleges in India: Admission Process

MBA admission process varies as per the college one is applying to. Below we have mentioned the basic stages one needs to go through while applying for MBA admission.

  • As most of the top MBA colleges in India 2023 accept admission on the basis of entrance exam scores, students first need to apply for the test accepted by their desired college.
  • Those qualifying for the MBA entrance exams are shortlisted for the admission process which generally includes Group Discussion (GD), Personal Interview (PI), and Written Ability Test (WAT). 
  • The admission is done on the basis of a candidate’s performance in the selection rounds, academic background, entrance exam score, etc. Those who get admission have to report to the college for fee payment and other formalities.

How to Select Top MBA Colleges in India?

If you are confused about which college to choose for MBA admission, then check out the points below to get some clarity.

  • Ranking & Affiliation Programmes: The ranking and affiliation of a college is a crucial factor that must be considered while seeking MBA admission. Before applying to the college, check its NIRF ranking, NAAC grade, and if it’s certified by either AICTE or AIU.
  • Courses Offered and Your Eligibility: It is necessary to have an idea of the courses offered by the colleges you wish to apply to. Also, it’s important to check whether you are eligible for that particular course.
  • Course Fees: The course fee is an essential factor that can’t be ignored when choosing the top MBA colleges in India. Know the cost of education beforehand so you can figure out the ways to afford the fees if it’s out of your budget.
  • Teacher-Student Ratio & Infrastructure: Make sure that the college you choose offers enough faculty in comparison to the ratio of students. Also, check whether the college has a good campus, favourable classroom environment, library, hostel facility, cafeteria, etc.
  • Placement Records: Before making a decision, check the placement percentage of the college, average salary, recruitment sectors, companies attending the campus recruitments, etc.

Top MBA Colleges in India 2023: Eligibility Criteria

Applicants must meet the following eligibility criteria for admission to top MBA colleges in India 2023.

  • Candidates must hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized board.
